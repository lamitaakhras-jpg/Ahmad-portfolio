Highway Wind Turbine — Senior Project

Lebanese International University | Spring 2024 - 2025

Senior project focused on designing a highway wind turbine system to generate renewable energy from vehicle-induced airflow. The project studied wind turbine concepts suitable for highway environments and focused on the use of a vertical-axis turbine design that can operate in turbulent wind conditions.

The project included research on horizontal wind turbines and Savonius wind turbines. The Savonius wind turbine was selected because it is simple, durable, low-maintenance, and capable of capturing wind from any direction, making it suitable for urban and highway applications.
